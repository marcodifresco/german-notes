# [[Modus - Indikativ]] - Präteritum
(Generally not formed for 'möchten' in its present-day usage, as it already implies a present wish. Past wishes are typically expressed with forms of 'wollen' or 'gerne haben'. If forced, it would derive from 'mögen' (mochte), but this changes the meaning from 'would like' to 'liked'). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Indikativ]] - Perfekt
(Not typically formed for 'möchten' in its present-day usage. Past wishes are typically expressed with forms of 'wollen' (gewollt haben) or 'gerne gehabt haben'. The Perfekt of 'mögen' is 'gemocht haben', changing the meaning). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Indikativ]] - Plusquamperfekt
(Not typically formed for 'möchten' in its present-day usage. Similar to Perfekt, past wishes are expressed with forms of 'wollen' (gewollt hatten) or 'gerne gehabt hatten'. The Plusquamperfekt of 'mögen' is 'gemocht hatten'). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Indikativ]] - Futur I
(Grammatically possible as 'werden möchten', but very uncommon and usually replaced by 'wollen werden' or alternative expressions for future wishes). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Indikativ]] - Futur II
(Not typically formed for 'möchten' in its present-day usage. Future perfect wishes are generally expressed using 'wollen' or alternative constructions). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 1 - Präsens
(Not typically formed for 'möchten', as 'möchten' itself is a Konjunktiv II form. For reported speech expressing a wish, Konjunktiv I of 'wollen' (wolle) is generally used). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 1 - Perfekt
(Not typically formed for 'möchten'). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 1 - Futur II

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 2 - Präteritum

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 2 - Futur I
(Grammatically possible as 'würde möchten', but highly redundant and not idiomatic, as 'möchten' already expresses the conditional/unreal. 'würde wollen' or similar constructions are preferred). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 2 - Futur II
(Not typically formed for 'möchten'). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Konjunktiv]] 2 - Plusquamperfekt
(Not typically formed for 'möchten'. 'Hätte gemocht' (from mögen) or 'hätte gewollt' (from wollen) would be used for past unreal wishes). Not typically formed

# [[Modus - Imperativ]] - Präsens
(The verb 'möchten' expresses a personal wish and therefore has no imperative forms). Not applicable

# [[Modus - Partizips]] - Präsens
(Not typically formed for 'möchten' in its modal-like usage. 'Mögend' is the Partizip Präsens of 'mögen'). Not applicable

# [[Modus - Partizips]] - Perfekt
(Not typically formed for 'möchten' in its modal-like usage. 'Gemocht' is the Partizip Perfekt of 'mögen'). Not applicable
